Chapter 3
The Sleepover




When they reached the dwarfs' house, Happy gave Sneezy a big red handkerchief to put on his nose. Sneezy kept on sneezing.

Dopey got the goldfish bowl and put it over, Sneezy's head. It worked! Sneezy stopped sneezing. But he couldn't get the bowl off his head! He tugged....and tugged....and tugged. It wouldn't come off! "Help!" he cried from inside the bowl.

Dopey looked around and saw the hay. He put some inside the bowl….. next to Sneezy's nose. "AH--AH--CHO0O" And the goldfish bowl flew right off Sneezys head.


Then it was time to eat dinner. While they were eating, Leona told Snow White and the dwarfs about home.

Later, when they went to sleep, Sneezy dreamed about the hay. His nose itched and twitched. "AH—CHOO!" He sneezed so loud that he woke everyone up except Sleepy. Leona took the big red handkerchief and tied it around Sneezy's nose. Grumpy gave everyone ear-muffs. And then they all went back to sleep.
